Joyce Zhang is currently a student at University of California, Riverside, where she studies Psychology and Education. After encountering encouraging teachers throughout her life, Joyce is determined to become one as well. She hopes that one day she will become a teacher who inspires and motivates students.

Starting at a young age, Joyce has been working with kids. With her parents always at work, she often had to take care of her younger brother and relatives. Throughout high school she volunteered to tutor kids, ranging from ages 6-11, in subjects such as Math and English. In addition, Joyce has spent her past summers working at KidTopia, where she creates activities for children to engage in. You can always trust Joyce and expect her to put in her best effort.

Joyce is a dog-lover and boba enthusiast. She is outgoing and extremely compassionate. You can always find her at the beach, reading, and watching the sun set.
